FAQs

  1. Q: I don't have any experience with Dim Sum, how does Dim Sum works?
    A: Usually, people start with tea, such as Jasmine tea, oolong tea, Chrysanthemum tea. Then, the servers push dim sum car around and ask you if you like anything. Just pick whatever you like and ask servers if you don't know.
  2. Q: What is Dim Sum?
    A: Dim Sum is a style of Chinese cuisine prepared as small bite-sized portions of food served in small steamer baskets or on small plates.
  3. Q: How much per person for the Dim Sum?
    A: Well, actually we don't serve dim sum as buffet, each dishes has its price. We prepare the best value dishes with its price.
